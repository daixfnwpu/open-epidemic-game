Epidemic

How to install on your Android device

First, join the Epidemic Testers group. If you're already a member all you have to do is follow this link:

https://play.google.com/apps/testing/com.declarative.games.epidemic.beta

You have two choices, either you can do this on your Android device or on your computer.

Doing this on your Android device

Follow the link

Tap on "Install"

Doing this on your computer

In order for this to work you will have be signed in on your computer on the same Google account that you are signed in on your phone

Click on "Become a Tester"

Install on a linked device

Click on "download it from the Play Store"

Now install it on a device attached to the Google account you're currently signed into.
